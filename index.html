Validate your French level with a DELF or DALF diploma

The DELF and DALF are official diplomas awarded by France's National Ministry of Education. They are valid for life and recognized around the world.
Candidates can register for the examination of their choice, according to their level. At each level, 4 skills are evaluated: listening, speaking, reading and writing.
The exam is open to all non native French speakers. Whether a diplomat, international business person, student of French interested in evaluating the progress of your language learning, or Francophone of any level curious about your French skills, this exam is for you.

Diplôme d'Études en Langue Française

It's a certification of French-language abilities for adult. It is composed of independent diplomas corresponding to the top four levels, A1, A2, B1 and B2.

Diplôme Approfondi de Langue Française

It's a certification of French-language abilities for non-native speakers administered. It is composed of independent diplomas corresponding to the top two levels, C1 and C2.

Diplôme d'Études en Langue Française Junior

The DELF junior is a diploma issued by the French Ministry of National Education in recognition of French-language studies. It can only be taken by children of secondary-level age.

Diplôme d'Études en Langue Française Prime

DELF Prim is the first level in the DELF / DALF series of certificates. DELF Prim is intended for children who are beginners in French as a Foreign Language
